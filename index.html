Main Courses

Masala Dosa

Ingredients:

  • Dosa batter: 2 cups
  • Potatoes: 2, boiled and mashed
  • Onions: 1, chopped
  • Mustard seeds: 1 tsp
  • Curry leaves: A few
  • Turmeric powder: 1/2 tsp
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, curry leaves, and onions.
  2. Add turmeric powder, salt, and mashed potatoes. Mix well.
  3. Spread dosa batter on a hot griddle and cook until golden.
  4. Place the potato filling inside the dosa and fold.
  5. Serve hot with sambar and coconut chutney.

Chole Bhature

Ingredients:

  • Chickpeas (Chole): 1 cup, soaked overnight
  • Onion: 1, chopped
  • Tomato puree: 1 cup
  • Ginger-garlic paste: 1 tbsp
  • Spices: Garam masala, turmeric, red chili powder, cumin
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil: 2 tbsp
  • For Bhature:
  • All-purpose flour (Maida): 2 cups
  • Yogurt: 1/2 cup
  • Baking soda: 1/2 tsp
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions:

  1. Pressure cook the soaked chickpeas until soft, and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add chopped onions, and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  4. Stir in the tomato puree, spices, and salt. Cook until the oil separates.
  5. Add the cooked chickpeas and simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  6. For Bhature, mix all-purpose flour, yogurt, baking soda, and salt to form a soft dough. Let it rest for 1 hour.
  7. Roll the dough into large circles and deep fry until golden brown.
  8. Serve the hot bhature with chole and enjoy!

Chicken Biryani

Ingredients:

  • Chicken: 500g
  • Basmati rice: 2 cups
  • Yogurt: 1/2 cup
  • Onions: 2, sliced
  • Ginger-garlic paste: 2 tbsp
  • Spices: Garam masala, turmeric, cumin, coriander
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt, spices, and salt for 1 hour.
  2. Cook onions until golden, add ginger-garlic paste and marinated chicken.
  3. Cook the chicken until done, then layer with partially cooked rice.
  4. Cover and cook on low heat until rice is fully cooked.
  5. Garnish with fried onions and serve hot.

Butter Chicken

Ingredients:

  • Chicken: 500g
  • Yogurt: 1 cup
  • Butter: 50g
  • Tomato puree: 1 cup
  • Cream: 1/2 cup
  • Spices: Garam masala, turmeric, red chili powder
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the chicken in yogurt, spices, and salt for 1 hour.
  2. Cook the marinated chicken in butter until golden brown.
  3. Add tomato puree and cook for another 10 minutes.
  4. Stir in cream, simmer for 5 minutes, and serve hot with naan or rice.

Paneer Tikka

Ingredients:

  • Paneer: 250g, cubed
  • Yogurt: 1/2 cup
  • Lemon juice: 1 tbsp
  • Spices: Garam masala, turmeric, red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Bell peppers and onions: 1 cup, cubed

Instructions:

  1. Mix yogurt, lemon juice, spices, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Add paneer, bell peppers, and onions to the marinade, and coat well.
  3. Marinate for 30 minutes.
  4. Grill or bake until paneer is golden and slightly charred.
  5. Serve hot with mint chutney.

Channa Masala

Ingredients:

  • Chickpeas: 2 cups (soaked overnight)
  • Onions: 2, finely chopped
  • Tomatoes: 2, pureed
  • Ginger-garlic paste: 1 tbsp
  • Spices: Garam masala, cumin, coriander, turmeric
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook soaked chickpeas in a pressure cooker until soft.
  2. In a pan, sauté onions until golden, then add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Add tomato puree and spices, cook until the oil separates.
  4. Add cooked chickpeas, mix well, and simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or bread.

Gulab Jamun

Ingredients:

  • Khoya: 200g
  • Maida (flour): 2 tbsp
  • Baking soda: A pinch
  • Sugar: 2 cups
  • Water: 1 cup
  • Cardamom powder: 1/2 tsp
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Mix khoya, maida, and baking soda to form a smooth dough.
  2. Shape the dough into small balls and fry until golden brown.
  3. Prepare sugar syrup by boiling sugar, water, and cardamom powder.
  4. Soak the fried balls in hot sugar syrup.
  5. Serve warm or at room temperature.

Rasmalai

Ingredients:

  • Milk: 1 liter
  • Lemon juice: 2 tbsp
  • Sugar: 1 cup
  • Water: 3 cups
  • Saffron: A few strands
  • Cardamom powder: 1/2 tsp
  • Almonds and pistachios: 2 tbsp, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Boil milk, add lemon juice to curdle it, and strain to get chhena (paneer).
  2. Knead the chhena into a smooth dough and shape into small, flat discs.
  3. Boil sugar and water to make syrup, add the discs, and cook until they expand and soften.
  4. In another pan, boil milk, saffron, and cardamom powder until thickened.
  5. Add the cooked discs to the thickened milk and refrigerate for a few hours.
  6. Garnish with chopped nuts and serve chilled.

Jalebi

Ingredients:

  • All-purpose flour (Maida): 1 cup
  • Yogurt: 1/2 cup
  • Water: As needed to make a batter
  • Sugar: 2 cups
  • Water: 1 cup
  • Saffron: A few strands
  • Cardamom powder: 1/2 tsp
  • Oil or ghee for frying

Instructions:

  1. Mix all-purpose flour, yogurt, and water to form a thick batter. Let it ferment for 6-8 hours.
  2. Prepare sugar syrup by boiling sugar, water, saffron, and cardamom powder until sticky.
  3. Heat oil or ghee in a pan. Pour the batter into a piping bag or squeeze bottle.
  4. Pipe the batter into hot oil in circular shapes and fry until golden and crispy.
  5. Immediately dip the fried jalebis in warm sugar syrup for a few seconds.
  6. Serve warm or at room temperature.

Chicken Pakoras

Ingredients:

  • Boneless chicken: 500g, cut into small pieces
  • Gram flour (besan): 1 cup
  • Ginger-garlic paste: 1 tbsp
  • Spices: Red chili powder, turmeric, cumin, coriander
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix chicken pieces with spices, ginger-garlic paste, and salt.
  2. Add gram flour and mix well to coat the chicken pieces.
  3. Heat oil in a deep pan and fry the chicken pieces until golden brown.
  4. Drain on paper towels and serve hot with chutney.

Carrot Halwa

Ingredients:

  • Carrots: 4, grated
  • Milk: 2 cups
  • Sugar: 1/2 cup
  • Ghee: 2 tbsp
  • Cardamom powder: 1/2 tsp
  • Cashews and raisins for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a pan, cook grated carrots in milk until soft and milk is absorbed.
  2. Add sugar and ghee, cook until the mixture thickens.
  3. Add cardamom powder and cook for a few more minutes.
  4. Garnish with cashews and raisins and serve warm.

  • Spices: Garam masala, turmeric, cumin, coriander
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions:

Cardamom Burfi

Ingredients:

  • Milk powder: 2 cups
  • Sugar: 1 cup
  • Ghee: 1/4 cup
  • Cardamom powder: 1/2 tsp
  • Milk: 1/2 cup
  • Pistachios for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a pan, mix milk powder, sugar, and milk. Cook on low heat until it thickens.
  2. Add ghee and cardamom powder, stirring continuously.
  3. When the mixture leaves the sides of the pan, pour it onto a greased plate.
  4. Spread evenly and garnish with pistachios. Let it cool, then cut into squares.
  5. Serve once fully set.

Grilled Chicken

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breasts: 4
  • Lemon juice: 2 tbsp
  • Olive oil: 2 tbsp
  • Garlic: 4 cloves, minced
  • Spices: Paprika, cumin, black pepper
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Marinate chicken breasts with lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, spices, and salt for 30 minutes.
  2. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  3. Grill chicken for 5-7 minutes on each side or until fully cooked.
  4. Serve hot with a side salad or vegetables.

Plum Cake

Ingredients:

  • All-purpose flour: 2 cups
  • Butter: 1 cup
  • Sugar: 1 cup
  • Eggs: 3
  • Dried fruits and nuts: 1 cup
  • Spices: Cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves
  • Vanilla extract: 1 tsp

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease and line a cake tin.
  2. Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy, then add eggs one at a time.
  3. Fold in flour, spices, dried fruits, nuts, and vanilla extract.
  4. Pour the batter into the prepared tin and bake for 45-50 minutes.
  5. Allow the cake to cool, then slice and serve.

Christmas Cookies

Ingredients:

  • All-purpose flour: 2 cups
  • Butter: 1 cup
  • Sugar: 1 cup
  • Egg: 1
  • Vanilla extract: 1 tsp
  • Colorful sprinkles for decoration

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Cream butter and sugar until fluffy, then add egg and vanilla extract.
  3. Gradually add flour and mix until a dough forms.
  4. Roll out the dough and cut into shapes using cookie cutters.
  5. Place on the baking sheet, decorate with sprinkles, and bake for 10-12 minutes.
  6. Allow to cool before serving or storing.
